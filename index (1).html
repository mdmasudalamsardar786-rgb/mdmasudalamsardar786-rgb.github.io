01

The Wall of Distance

If the distance were shorter, If I could come to you whenever my heart desired, How happy I would be We would sit together for endless chats, Share cups of tea, And lose ourselves in stories, Laughter echoing between sips and smiles. But this distance It stands like a wall between us, A silent pause in our togetherness. So I must find a way, A way to close the space between us soon, Because my heart can't wait much longer To be near you again.

02

The Light of Happiness

It never shouts, nor is it a blinding brightness silent, calm, yet profound. In the middle of ordinary days, suddenly it can be found in a gentle smile, in a moment that softly cuts into the heart. A ray of light—perhaps bright, perhaps faint, yet the search continues every day. The fear of losing it, a bond drawn by an endless, invisible pull. A single drop of light within the darkness reminds me if you are searching for me, begin by searching within yourself.

03

Long Walk

This path moves forward Like the sky that never fades away, Silently, it moves on—unceasing, Like the unseen flow of air, Always striving to reach the destination. Even if time comes to an end, Like the cover of a worn-out book, Still, effortlessly I search anew, With tireless effort and endless hope, For that one true destination. Through the long road of the past, I leave behind moments in silence, Following the structure of time, As if it gently leads me To the purpose of my journey's end.
